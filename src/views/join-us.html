Join Us!

Become a Contributor

We're always looking for developers to help improve Ano.js. Ano.js is powered by open source contributions, so the best way is to become a contributor on this project, and that can be done just by filling out the form below!

Benefits of being a Contributor
  • Paste it on your LinkedIn
  • Great work experience to show
  • Add to your portfolio/resume
  • Show off Ano.js to future employers
  • No time constraints or commitments
  • You choose how much you contribute
  • Receive recognition
  • More contributions = more recognition
  • Collaborators with the most amount of contributions are shown the most
  • You receive credit for animations you create
  • Eligible for staff positions
  • Gain experience
  • Learn more about software engineering
  • Network with fellow collaborators
Get Invited!

After submitting this form, you will receive an invitation in your email to become a contributor on the Ano.js Animations GitHub repository.

If you do not get an invite, please double check that you have typed in your GitHub username correctly.

NOTE: Make sure you're logged in before you accept the invitation.
