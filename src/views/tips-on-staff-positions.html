Tips on Getting a Staff Position at Ano.js

What you should know:

  • Basic Programming
  • Basic Frontend Web Development (HTML/CSS/JS)
  • Most/All of the requirements of staff position
  • Leadership

What you should do:

  • Be active on Slack and GitHub
  • Be responsive and proactive
  • Respond to most/all Slack messages concerning you
  • Work well with peers

Extra Bonuses:

  • Refer people you know to use Ano.js
  • Refer people you know to become an Ano.js contributor
  • Show off Ano.js on social media
  • Encourage your peers to be productive
