Our Story

The idea of Ano.js was birthed by Calix Huang, the creator, a couple days before a hackathon, aiming to host web animations for others to use, because he was learning web animation with HTML5 Canvas and Javascript. This idea was brought into action in a hackathon called Atoms & Bits hosted at Make School in San Francisco. Ano.js was built by Calix Huang, Udhav Sharma, Srikiran Pothama, and Gio Rocchio, with Calix as the lead developer, Udhav and Srikiran as business and financial analysts, and Gio as the web administrator.

Ano.js ended up winning Best Website and first place, and the idea was later improved on by Calix Huang, who is now taking on the responsiblility of updating and maintaing Ano.js.
